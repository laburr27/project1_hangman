Hangman

You're Hanging By Our Every Word!

Rules:

Guess a letter. If the letter is in the word, it'll be shown in the blanks. If you think you know the word, you can guess it at any time. For each wrong guess, a body part will be added to the gallows. You have five guesses- one for the head, neck, arms, torso, and legs. And remember- if you lose, don't get too hung up on it! Good luck!
